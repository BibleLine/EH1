The Love Definition is a poem inspired by 1 Corinthians 4-8 in 2025. The Love Definition takes the verse and
gives it a personal perspective on when and
how each attribute of love is used.


The love Definition

Love is patient,

Even though we can be complacent.

Love never fails, nor does it fade,

it is always there, even when you're afraid.

Love endures all things without weakening,

even when we can be very self-seeking.

Love is neither jealous nor rudefull,

instead, it is kind and thoughtful.

So if you are ever in a bind,

God's warm love is what you will always find.

Who is He?

Who is He who gives breath to the lions,

who dresses and colors the dandelions,

who counts every grain of sand

and knows every single piece of land.

Is there someone greater than He,

someone who can do greater than Thee?

Who can also make the blind see

And part open a great sea.

There is none like our Jireh,

for His name is Yeshua the Messiah.
